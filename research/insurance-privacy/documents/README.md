# Document Archive

This folder contains the member-facing privacy documents analyzed in the
**Health Insurance Privacy and Patient Agency** study.

The documents were collected directly from publicly accessible insurer
websites between **June 13 and June 17, 2026** and represent the versions
available at the time of analysis.

## Contents

The archive includes:

- HIPAA Notices of Privacy Practices
- Web Privacy Statements
- Data Sharing Notices
- Gramm–Leach–Bliley (GLB) Privacy Notices
- Global Health Benefits Privacy Notices

for five major insurers operating in Georgia.

## Document Inventory

See **`document_log.csv`** for:

- insurer
- document type
- filename
- source URL
- access date
- reported policy update date

## Reproducibility

Privacy policies may change over time without notice. This archive
preserves the versions analyzed in the study to support research
reproducibility.
